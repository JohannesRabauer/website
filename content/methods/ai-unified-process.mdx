## What it is

AI Unified Process is a spec-first workflow. You write the business intent first. AI then turns
that intent into architecture, code, tests, and release work.

It uses four phases: Inception, Elaboration, Construction, and Transition. Each phase has its own
outputs and its own purpose.

## Why it matters

The spec stays in charge. That makes change easier to manage. When the requirements change, you
update the spec and let AI rebuild the parts below it.
