## What it is

Semantic Anchors are public terms you can say to an LLM and expect a useful result. The term
pulls in a known pattern. For example, arc42 points to an architecture template, BLUF means lead
with the answer, and Gherkin points to acceptance tests.

## What contracts add

Contracts are local. They tell the team what a term means in this repository. That keeps the
anchor useful and keeps the output consistent.
