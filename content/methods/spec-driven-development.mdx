## What it is

Spec-driven development keeps the spec alive. The spec says what the system should do. It does
not lock in the framework or the database. That keeps the document focused on business intent.

## Why it matters

When behavior changes, update the spec first. Then regenerate the code. That keeps the spec useful
instead of stale.
