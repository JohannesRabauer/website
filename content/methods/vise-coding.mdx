## What it is

Vise Coding keeps the change between two jaws. One jaw is the spec. The other is the quality
gate. The agent works in the middle. It moves forward only when the step is small and the checks
pass.

## Why it matters

This keeps review simple. It also catches drift early. The approach works best when you already
have real tests and static checks.
