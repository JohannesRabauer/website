## What it is

Guided Coding keeps the plan, the limits, and the review rules visible. You write the guide
before the code. The guide tells the agent what to do and what not to do.

## Why it matters

This keeps the human in control. It also makes review easier, because you compare the code against
the guide instead of against memory.
